The point of a life note here is not daily broadcasting. It is keeping a human scale around long-term technical work.

![Weekly reset cover](/images/life/weekly-reset.svg)

The weekly reset looks at three simple questions:

1. what input is still worth reading,
2. what project is truly moving,
3. and what task is only consuming attention.
