## Why this exists

Many personal sites stop at one-time presentation. The more durable version is a site that keeps absorbing writing, research, projects, and a small amount of life context over time.

![MyWeb cover](/images/projects/myweb-cover.svg)

## Current direction

The first version uses Astro, MDX, Tailwind, and static deployment. The bilingual pairing is stabilized through `translationKey`, not hard-coded URLs alone.
