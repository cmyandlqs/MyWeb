A weak research note repeats the paper. A useful one reconnects results to your own task constraints.

![RF-DETR note cover](/images/research/rf-detr-note.svg)

The note here focuses on:

* what problem the model actually solves,
* under which conditions the reported gains matter,
* and what assumptions may break when moved to a new dataset.
